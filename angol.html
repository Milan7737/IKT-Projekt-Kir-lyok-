The world of weapons

Introduction

Our websites are about the evolution of weapons and the weapons of World War 2. In our main page we wrote about the history of weapons and how we got to modern day wartare.

We made 4 separate pages for 4 different countries about their World War 2 weapons, tanks and aircrafts. The first country is Germany where we wrote about the MP-40 (gun), the He-1/7 Greif (aircraft) and the infamous Tiger (tank). Our second country was the USA. We collected information about the M1919 (gun), the M4 Sherman (tank), and the P-51 Mustang (aircraft). Next is the Soviet Union where we detailed the SZVT-40 (gun), T-34 (tank), and the 1-16 (aircraft), The last country we wrote about was the United Kingdom where we mentioned the Vikers (gun), the Crusader (tank) and the Supermarine Spitfire (aircraft). We also made a page with additional tanks where we mentioned Italian, French, Swedish and Hungarian ones.
